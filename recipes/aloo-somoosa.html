Aloo Somoosa

Description

This is a very simple recipe to make, assuming you have the Somoosa patties already made. You just put all the spices in a pan first, stir them around. Then you add the potatoes. Stir around a little more and you have your filling.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes 3 boiled and chopped
  • Cumin seed 1 tsp
  • Coriander seed 1 tsp
  • Garlic chopped 1 tbsp
  • Green chili chopped 1-2
  • Onion Chopped 1 cup
  • Salt 1 tsp
  • Red chili powder 1 tsp
  • Turmeric Powder 0.5 tsp
  • Red chili flakes 1 tsp
  • Lemon Juice 1 tbsp

Steps:

  1. Put 2 tablespoons of oil in a pan. Heat them up a little and then put your cumin seeds in it.
  2. Then put the onions in and stir around a little.
  3. One by one put in all the rest of the spices. And fry for 2 minutes.
  4. Then put in your potatoes and mix it well with all the spices.
  5. Now take a somoosa strip and make it into a somoosa packet.
  6. Put in the filling and close the pocket.
  7. Fry them and voila!
